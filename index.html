Hamid's Biography

Hamid is a mysterious figure known for his deep knowledge and adventurous spirit. With a background in technology and a love for the outdoors, Hamid has traveled to over 50 countries, always seeking new challenges.

From his early days of learning to code by the flicker of a campfire to becoming an international chess grandmaster, Hamid's journey has been nothing short of remarkable.
